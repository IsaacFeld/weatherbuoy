Want to know when you can finally take a swim? Using our custom mailing system you can setup temperature alerts. Choose a body of water, a temperature, and enter your email. Once submitted, whenever that temperature is reached, you’ll be the first to know. Alert Types You can choose between instant alerts and accurate alerts. Instant alerts will be sent as soon as the temperature is reached, whereas accurate alerts will come out if the target temperature has been reached for 7 consecutive days, providing more accuracy.
